# Raphaël's review — tezos-x-octez-connect

  ## Overall verdict
  Positive. The solution looks reasonable to him: contained impact on wallets and dApps, real UX gain, and he thinks it's cool that it's backward-compatible.

  ## Issues with the presentation site

  ### Navigation / structure
  - Navigation not very intuitive: hard to tell the proposal description apart from the validation plan.
  - The dApp / Chrome extension / Standalone app tabs are easy to miss (he missed them on first pass).
  - The 7-step plan (all marked "done") takes up too much space on the homepage — should be removed, along with the "Technical details" page.
  - No link to the demo found on the site (he only came across the video via Slack).

  ### Inconsistency to clarify
  - The header advertises "PostMessage, Matrix P2P, and WalletConnect v2", but the Standalone app page mentions "Matrix P2P transport" and "Popup transport (new)". Unclear whether
  the "popup" case covers PostMessage + WalletConnect or if it's something else entirely.

  ## Main gap
  - A new TZIP extending TZIP-10.
